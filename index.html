about me

I am an engineering student based in Stuttgart Area, Germany. Currently I am working on my master thesis at the Daimler Bosch cooperation for autonomous driving. After my graduation in October I plan to start a PhD.

Soon I will obtain my master degree in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology. During my studies, I focused on machine learning, primary for autonomous driving applications. While being with Daimler, I worked with LiDAR sensors for environment perception and started my master thesis on intelligent LiDAR data up-sampling with Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs). I intend to keep working in the deep learning field for self-driving cars and plan to become a PhD student, exploring the endless possibilities of artificial intelligence.
