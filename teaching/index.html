teaching

Materials for courses that I taught.

I teach the course Advanced Visual Processing at the University of Stuttgart since 2024. The course is a combination of classical image processing and modern computer vision.

Lecture materials are available upon registration to the course.

For a little impression, check out my weekly lecture summaries from the summer semester 2024:

  • Pinhole camera vs. lens camera
  • Camera matrix and calibration
  • Fourier transformation
  • Image operations and filtering
  • Feature extraction and matching
  • Edge detection
  • Image segmentation
  • 3D estimation
  • Image classification
  • Neural networks
  • Deep learning
